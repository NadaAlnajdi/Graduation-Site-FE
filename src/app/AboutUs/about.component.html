Our Problem

Patients with Cut in the arm, wheelchair for the disabled, car, toys and smart home Led to paralysis (unable to move their limbs and have difficulties in facial muscles as well) which affected badly on their abilities to communicate with others and usage their smart devices.

Why we Choose It ?

We choose it according to Statistics, Paralysis is dramatically more widespread than previously thought and it has effect on the most important aspect in our daily life (communication) that satisfy the belonging needs of human being therefore we put the problem in our consideration and seek for solving it, beside that we intend to provide comfort environment for our patient as controlling his smart home by his own brain.

Our Solution Abstraction

We made a project to control the human mind through the headset, receiving a signal from the EEG and then sending it to the microcontroller in order to read and translate the event of each application to operate the arm and move it in the 4 directions, operate the wheelchair so that the man who is unable to move can move using the headset, also can drive his car and by receiving signals and using automation he can control the smart home and operate any device, also the headset had a role in menâ€™s exercise and games.
