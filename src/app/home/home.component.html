Welcome to Our Team

People with an amputated arm, or using a wheelchair, disabled who can’t drive cars, play with toys, or even manage their homes, anything related to paralysis (unable to move their limbs and have difficulties in facial muscles as well) which affected badly on their abilities to communicate with others and using their smart devices.

Paralysis is dramatically more widespread than previously thought and it affects the most important aspect of our daily life (communication) that satisfy the belonging needs of human being therefore we put the problem into our consideration and seek for solving it, besides that we intend to provide a comfortable environment for our patient as controlling his smart home by his brain.

OUR APPLICATIONS

We used AI and IOT techniques to make a project which gets signals from the human mind through the headset, receiving a signal from the EEG headset and then sending it to the microcontroller to read and translate the event of each application

Robot car

we use a brain-computer interface (BCI) which is connected to our autonomous car. The car is equipped with a variety of sensors and can be controlled by a computer.

Game

we don’t forget how fun is important in our life so we worked on developing a game that can be played by your EEG signals

Smart Home

By receiving signals and using automation you can control appliances at your home by just thinking of them
